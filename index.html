VIKING BRYTING INC.

VIKING BRYTING INC. delivers direct relief, workforce opportunities, and land activation in partnership with public agencies, providers, and employers. All programs are designed to be transparent and measurable.

Relief & Navigation

We operate outreach, food & hygiene, and navigation-to-services programs with trusted partners. Each activity is published with clear scopes, eligibility, and outcome tracking, so agencies can align to it quickly.

Workforce & Employer Links

We run paid transitional crews, certifications, and employer introductions that lead to stable income. Our teams prioritize retention and benefits over short-term counts; public MOUs clarify roles between VIKING BRYTING INC., employers, and city partners.

Land & Housing Activation

We help reclaim underused parcels for interim safe sites and create clear pathways to permanent housing. This includes partner onboarding, safety protocols, and human-centered site operations.
